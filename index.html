How It Works

Simple. Trusted. Effective.

Getting care and companionship is as easy as a few taps on our app.

1

User Registration

Sign up on the CareSync+ app or website and select your required service and preferred companion type.

2

Companion Onboarding

Every companion registers with ID verification, police background check, and professional training.

3

Service Matching

AI-enabled system matches users with suitable companion partners based on location, availability, and needs.

4

Service Delivery

Companion provides emotional, wellness, or hospital support with real-time tracking through the app.

Payment & Feedback
